关于我们

我们致力于 为您提供良好的出行居住体验

作为业内知名的品牌，景明酒店及度假村是时尚现代并高瞻远瞩的全球酒店业典范。凭借对产品、设施和 服务的不断创新，拥有 92 年历史的景明将继续引领行业的发展方向。我们利用智能设计、创新餐厅理念、热情周到的服务以及对全球社区的承诺，让旅行变得更加轻松。景明目前所服务 的国家/地区超过任何提供全方位综合性服务的其他酒店品牌，在遍及六大洲的 79 个国家/地区拥有 570 多家酒店及度假酒店。

As a well-known brand in the industry, Jingming Hotel and resort is a modern and forward-looking model of the Global Hotel Industry. With a 92 year history of innovation in products, facilities and services, Keimei will continue to lead the industry. We use smart design, innovative restaurant ideas, warm and thoughtful service, and a commitment to the global community to make travel easier. Keimei currently serves, more countries and territories than any other hotel brand offering a full range of integrated services, with more than 570 hotels and resorts in 79 countries and territories on six continents.
